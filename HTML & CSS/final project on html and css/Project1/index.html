Curated Event Experiences

Celebrating over a decade of service, Dreamcraft Events is a boutique event planning and design company that specializes in nonprofit fundraising, conferences, and annual celebrations.

We are inspired by our clients’ mission, values, and goals to create memorable experiences and cultivate lasting impressions and impact. From spreadsheets to illustrated activations, let us help share your vision and build your dream event.

What we bring to the table...

Planning With over a decade of experience, leave the spreadsheets, budget management, vendor outreach, mobile bidding software, and onsite event management to us.

design From event storyboarding to marketing, our team will design your dream event from the ground up.

Production Stage management is our forte. Working with our preferred production partners, we will ensure that every light, sound, and moment is perfectly on cue!

FulFillment You bring the sponsors and we'll handle the rest! If you want to ensure full engagement of sponsors at your event and you want them to keep coming back year after year, lean on our fulfillment services to keep those VIPs happy!
