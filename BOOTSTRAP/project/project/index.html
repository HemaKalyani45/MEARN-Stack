About Us

BaathKhaana is a vibrant food court in Miyapur, Hyderabad, offering a perfect blend of delicious food, warm hospitality, and a welcoming ambiance. From authentic Indian flavors to popular favorites, we serve quality dishes that bring people together. Whether it's a family dinner, a casual outing, or a special celebration, BaathKhaana is the place to create memorable dining experiences.
